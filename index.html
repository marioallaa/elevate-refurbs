FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

At Elevate Refurbs, we understand that home renovation projects can raise many questions. 
To help you navigate the process with ease, we’ve compiled a list of frequently asked questions (FAQs). From understanding our services to project timelines, these answers will give you a clear idea of what to expect when working with us. 

Whether you’re planning a loft conversion, kitchen upgrade, or full home refurbishment, we’re here to make your renovation experience smooth and hassle-free.

What areas do you serve?

At Elevate Refurbs, we operate across Greater London, ensuring top-quality refurbishment services wherever you are. Our commitment to delivering exceptional craftsmanship and personalized service has made us a trusted choice for homeowners in these areas and beyond. Whether you're looking to upgrade your kitchen, convert your loft, or embark on a full home renovation, we're here to help you elevate your living space. 
While we serve clients throughout the city, most of our customers come from

  • Barnet
  • Kensington and Chelsea
  • Camden
  • Hammersmith and Fulham
  • Harrow
What refurbishment services do you offer?

We offer a full range of home refurbishment services, including loft conversions, kitchen and bathroom upgrades, home extensions, and bespoke carpentry. Additionally, we handle electrical work, plumbing, painting, and decorating to provide a comprehensive solution for all your renovation requirements.

Do you manage electrical and plumbing work as part of the refurbishment?

Yes, we manage all aspects of electrical and plumbing work. From rewiring your home to installing new plumbing systems, our team ensures every aspect of the refurbishment is completed to the highest safety and quality standards.

How long does a typical refurbishment project take?

The timeline for each project depends on its size and complexity. For example, bathroom refurbishments might take a few weeks, while full home renovations or extensions can take several months. We provide a detailed timeline during the initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

Will I have a dedicated project manager?

Yes, every project we undertake is assigned a dedicated project manager. This ensures smooth communication, timely project updates, and a single point of contact to oversee your renovation from start to finish.

Do you provide painting and decorating services?

Yes, painting and decorating are included in our services. We offer expert advice on color schemes and finishes to ensure the final look of your home is exactly what you envision, with high-quality materials and precise execution.

How do I get a quote for my refurbishment project?

To receive a free quote, simply contact us through our website, phone, or email. We’ll arrange an on-site consultation to assess your property, understand your needs, and provide a detailed, transparent estimate.
